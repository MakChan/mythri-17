7th Annual Sports Meet 2017

September 26th - 28th

A Few Words About Us

JSS Academy of Technical Education will be celebrating its 7th Annual Sports Meet from 26th-28th of September in the college premises.

MYTHRI (Mythri means - Friendship) is the annual sports meet of JSSATE Noida. The aim of this sports meet is to bring students and faculty together outside the classroom to bond. This year we are going to celebrate the 7th Annual Sports Meet MYTHRI 2017 to be held from 26th to 28th September 2017
